AssistantX is a cutting-edge LLM-powered assistant designed to enhance office productivity by proactively managing tasks in real-world office environments. Leveraging advanced language models, AssistantX offers seamless integration and automation capabilities tailored to office settings.


AssistantX is designed to handle a wide range of office tasks in a proactive manner, offering innovative solutions and automation to improve efficiency and productivity in real-world office environments.

Abstract

Imagine having a capable assistant; you would naturally expect it to handle various tasks on your behalf. For instance, if you wish to print a file but lack access to a printer, your expectation is simply to send the file to the assistant, which will manage the rest—locating someone with a printer, requesting them to print it, and ultimately returning the printed document to you. During this process, you won’t be concerned about what transpires beyond receiving the printed file. Any uncertainties or challenges can be autonomously addressed by the assistant itself. Unfortunately, current service robots fall short of such expectations due to their limited inference and response capabilities in navigating dynamic uncertainties within real-world environments. This inadequacy motivates us to develop AssistantX: an LLM-powered embodied agent designed for clarifying user instructions, exploring physical environments, and communicating with other team members for assistance (see Fig.1). AssistantX is implemented using a multi-agent architecture that can also be easily adapted for use in other environments.
