Abstract

AssistantX represents a significant advancement in office automation and productivity. By leveraging state-of-the-art large language models (LLMs), AssistantX provides a proactive approach to managing and automating office tasks. Unlike traditional systems that react to user input, AssistantX anticipates needs and proactively performs tasks based on context and predefined goals. This approach not only enhances productivity but also ensures a smoother workflow in office environments. Our evaluation demonstrates AssistantX's effectiveness in various real-world scenarios, highlighting its capability to handle diverse tasks and adapt to different office settings with minimal intervention.
